How licensing works

  1. Buy on the storefront; you receive a key like ts_live_….
  2. Run dietcode license activate ts_live_….
  3. The CLI verifies against the license server, caches the result to ~/.config/dietcode/license.json, and honors a 7-day offline grace window.
