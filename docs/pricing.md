## Why pay

The free tier teaches the behavior. Pro buys:

- **One CLI for every host** — skip the per-editor setup.
- **`ultra` mode + review skill** — measurably smaller diffs on real work.
- **Updates** — rules are re-tuned as models change.

## How licensing works

1. Buy on the storefront; you receive a key like `ts_live_…`.
2. Run `dietcode license activate ts_live_…`.
3. The CLI verifies against the license server, caches the result to `~/.config/dietcode/license.json`, and honors a 7-day offline grace window.

See [`configuration.md`](./configuration.md) for the verify contract and environment variables.
